**Task**: Generate a brief summary of a text chunk for chapter planning.

---

## Instructions

You are a content analyst. The following is a chunk of transcript text.

**Do the following**:

1. Read the entire text carefully
2. Write a 1-2 sentence summary capturing the **main topic(s)** discussed
3. Note any clear topic transitions

**Do NOT**:
- Translate content
- Structure or format the text
- Include direct quotes
- Write more than 3 sentences

**Output**: Summary only. No explanations.
